Welcome to the Brighte Green Loan

The Brighte Green Loan is a fixed interest rate, structured the same way as a personal loan, and is used to finance energy efficient products such as solar, batteries, solar hot water, and solar heating and cooling.

About the Brighte Green Loan

  • A fixed interest rate

  • Fund purchases between
    $1k to $30K

  • Repay over 2 to 7 years

  • Make additional repayments

Am I eligible to apply for a Brighte Green Loan?
To be eligible to apply you must:

  • Be over 18 years old and an Australian resident or citizen

  • Own or be purchasing a home

  • Be employed, self-employed, a self-funded retiree; or is receiving the Government Age Pension

  • Have an Australian driver’s license or Passport or Medicare Card

  • Provide two most recent payslips or 90 days of bank statements
